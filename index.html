My name is Alex Skaldsson. I'm a nature and wildlife photographer. My work is focused on empty, open spaces. Mountains, snowy fields and peaks, somewhere you can go and find refuge from it all.

The Photographer

I love photography and have worked as a commercial photographer for the last fifteen years, but have now decided to shoot for pleasure instead of just for work and regain the love I once had for this medium, before it became a job. Follow me in this journey.
