Wide landscapes that breathe.

My name is Alex Skaldsson. I'm a nature and wildlife photographer. My work is focused on empty, open spaces. Mountains, snowy fields and peaks, somewhere you can go and find refuge from it all.

The Photographer

I have spent the last fifteen years working as a commercial photographer. Now, I am dedicating my time to personal work. This portfolio is a return to why I first picked up a camera: the pure, unfiltered pleasure of capturing the world around me. Thank you for following along.
