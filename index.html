About

Alex Skaldsson is a nature photographer focused on landscape, atmosphere, and the relationship between light and terrain. His work explores quiet environments, subtle contrast, and a restrained visual language.

The overlapping sections are there to make the portfolio feel more current and editorial while keeping the typography restrained and the overall structure minimal.
