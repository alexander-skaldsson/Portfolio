Photography with Purpose.

Art is more than observation; it is a tool for change. This space is dedicated to initiatives that use the lens to support vulnerable communities and drive real impact.

The Reality: Child Poverty in Spain

Despite being a strong economy, Spain is facing a structural crisis that often goes unseen. Economic growth has not protected the most vulnerable, and the statistics are a sobering call to action.

"Currently, almost 1 in 3 children in Spain is at risk of poverty or social exclusion."

This represents over 2 million minors whose futures, health, and educational opportunities are severely compromised. It is an intergenerational cycle of financial exclusion that requires an active response from all sectors of society, including the arts.

The Project: Miradas de Calatayud

Since moving to Spain, the beauty of Calatayud has profoundly inspired my work. "Miradas de Calatayud: Arte por la Infancia" is an upcoming charity exhibition designed to turn that beauty into tangible help.

The concept is entirely transparent: I am donating my time, my portfolio, and the rights to these images for this event. 100% of the net profits from the sale of the artwork will be donated directly to Save the Children Spain to fund their vital programs combating child poverty.

Save the Children has officially backed this initiative, and their logo will proudly be displayed as the sole beneficiary of the funds raised.
